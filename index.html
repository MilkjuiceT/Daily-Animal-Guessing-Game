Animal Guesser

Welcome to Animal Guesser! Each day will feature a daily animal. Your job is to guess several pieces of information about that animal.

1 Drag each slider to your best guess on what the animal is. Then select each continent where you think the animal is located.
2 The closer your answers are to being correct, the more points you'll receive. Each category awards up to 100 points, with a maximum of 500 points for the entire game. Good luck!
